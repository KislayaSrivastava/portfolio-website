About Me

15+ years evolving from developer to cloud architect

I'm a Cloud Solutions Architect with a passion for solving complex infrastructure challenges through elegant, scalable solutions. Over 15 years, I've evolved from building legacy applications to architecting cloud-native systems that serve thousands of users globally.

My expertise lies in AWS cloud architecture, Infrastructure-as-Code (Terraform, CloudFormation), and DevOps practices. I've led successful cloud migrations for 50+ enterprise customers, achieving significant cost reductions and performance improvements through strategic architecture decisions.

What drives me is the intersection of technology and business value. I don't just design systems—I architect solutions that directly impact the bottom line. Whether it's reducing infrastructure costs by 40% or improving deployment efficiency by 95%, I focus on measurable outcomes.
